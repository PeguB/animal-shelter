About Us

At Lucky Paws Rescue, we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our furry friends. With a passionate team and a commitment to finding loving homes, we strive to create a brighter future for every animal that comes our way.

Our ultimate joy comes from seeing the smiling faces of our happy customers who have found their perfect companions, forging lifelong bonds and bringing immeasurable joy to their lives. Join our community of compassionate individuals and be a part of our mission to transform lives, one adoption at a time.

Our cute dogs

Our lovable dogs are patiently waiting for their forever homes. From playful pups to gentle seniors, each dog in our adoption center is a unique and faithful companion, ready to fill your life with boundless love and loyalty. Whether you're looking for an energetic partner for outdoor adventures or a snugly friend for cozy nights, our dogs are eager to bring joy and happiness to your home.

Our sweet cats

Our adorable cats are eagerly awaiting their chance to find a loving family. With their independent spirits and affectionate nature, our feline friends offer companionship and comfort in equal measure. If you're seeking a purring presence and a lifelong friend, our cats are ready to charm their way into your heart and home.

Caring for your pets

Caring for our pets is at the heart of everything we do. At our adoption center, we provide a range of top-notch facilities to ensure the well-being and happiness of our animals. Our open playground offers a safe and stimulating environment for them to socialize, exercise, and play. Our dedicated and loving staff members are always there to look for them with affection, attention, and care. To ensure their health and medical needs are met, we have a skilled veterinarian who provides expert care for our adopted animals. With these exceptional facilities and a commitment to their welfare, we strive to give our beloved pets the utmost comfort and attention they deserve.

Our Location

Lucky Paws Rescue center is situated within close proximity to the city's heart, in Unirii square, in order to offer a convenient and accessible space for everyone. To accommodate our visitors' busy schedules, we keep our doors open all week long. You can visit our adoption center any day of the week to meet and interact with our adorable animals. We understand the importance of finding the right match and creating a comfortable environment for both the animals and potential adopters. With our accessible location and generous opening hours, we aim to provide a welcoming space where people can embark on their journey of adopting a lifelong companion.
